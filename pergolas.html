Looking to add some structure and architecture to your backyard while blending the project into an existing living space? A pergola can be a great place to start!


Generally, a pergola is a wide-open structure with four support beams, no walls, and a decorative roof design, such as a lattice. Commonly used for shade or even a touch of shelter from the elements, options are available to add a variety of customizable features such as screens that can be added to the roof or walls and strands of lights to decorate for evening and night time.
